Spartacus

About the series

The television series 'Spartacus,' which aired from 2010 to 2013, is a modern retelling of the story of the titular Thracian gladiator who led a slave revolt against the Roman Republic. Created by Steven S. DeKnight, the series blends historical events with dramatic storytelling, intense action, and visceral visuals.
